Who We Are

Madhyabindu Pvt. Ltd. is a premier industrial contractor serving Nepal's heavy industry sector. We specialize in turning complex engineering challenges into structural realities. With a focus on cement factories and heavy manufacturing plants, we provide end-to-end solutions from fabrication to erection and dismantling.

Director's Message

Mr. Rajesh Dumre

With decades of hands-on experience in the industrial sector, Mr. Rajesh Dumre has steered Madhyabindu Pvt. Ltd. to become a trusted name among cement manufacturers. His philosophy is simple: never compromise on the quality of steel or the safety of a worker.

"In our line of work, precision isn't a luxury; it's a necessity. Whether we are erecting a massive chimney or dismantling a retired unit, we treat every bolt and beam with the same level of respect and attention to detail. Our client's trust is our strongest structure."
